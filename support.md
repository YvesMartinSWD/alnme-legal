## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting started

**Is there a free trial?**
Yes. AL n Me is free for 90 days from your first launch. No credit card required.
After 90 days, a subscription is required for Tasks, Events, and Travel.
Shopping is always free.

**Do I need an account?**
No. AL n Me works entirely without an account. Your data stays on your device.

---

### Your data

**Where is my data stored?**
Everything is stored locally on your iPhone. Nothing is sent to our servers —
we don't have any. Your data never leaves your device except for optional iCloud backup
(which goes to your own iCloud account, not ours).

**How do I back up my data?**
Go to Dashboard → Backup → Save to iCloud. Your backup is saved to your personal
iCloud account. You can also save to Files for a local backup.

**How do I move my data to a new phone?**
Create a backup on your old device (Dashboard → Backup), then restore it on your
new device after installing AL n Me.

**Can I share lists with someone else?**
Yes. Open any list, tap the share icon, and send the encrypted `.alnme` file through any
messaging app (Messages, WhatsApp, email, etc.). The recipient imports it directly
into their AL n Me. No shared account needed.

---

### Subscriptions

**What does the subscription include?**
A single subscription unlocks Tasks, Events, and Travel features on all your devices.
Shopping is always free, with or without a subscription.

**How do I restore my subscription on a new device?**
Go to Dashboard → Backup tab → Restore Purchases.

**How do I cancel?**
Open the iOS Settings app → Apple ID → Subscriptions → AL n Me → Cancel.
Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

---

### Permissions

**Why does the app need Location access?**
To show nearby shops on the map and send you a notification when you arrive at a
shop that has items on your list. Location is used on-device only — it is never
sent to us.

**Why does the app need Microphone / Speech Recognition?**
For the voice assistant feature. Voice is processed on your device by Apple's
speech engine. Audio is never recorded or stored.

**Can I use the app without giving any permissions?**
Yes. All permissions are optional. You can deny everything and still use the full
app — you simply won't have map, voice, or geofence notification features.

---

### Technical

**The app is not responding / crashing**
1. Force-close the app and reopen it
2. Restart your device
3. If the issue persists, email us at support@smartinnovationforyou.com with your iOS version and device model

**I lost my data**
If you have a backup, go to Dashboard → Backup → Restore and select your most
recent backup file.

**How do I import a `.alnme` file sent by someone?**
Tap the `.alnme` file attachment in any app (Messages, Mail, Files, etc.). AL n Me
opens automatically and shows you a preview before importing.
